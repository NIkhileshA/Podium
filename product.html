PRODUCT

Interact with with every customer from one, effective platform.

Transform the way you manage your online reputation.

Podium brings your business’ reputation, from across the web, into one centralized place where you can effectively build and manage your online reviews from multiple sites. With Podium, it’s easy to see your portfolio of online reviews to make faster, better-informed decisions.

Collect up to 15x more reviews from your happy customers, while in your place of business.

Normally, collecting reviews is hard. But, with Podium, you can easily collect 100s of reviews utilizing text message, your customer’s smartphones, and apps with which they are most familiar. Podium’s mobile technology ensures you’re maximizing your online reviews through an efficient, mobile process that yields up to 15x the reviews that traditional platforms produce.

Bring all of your online reviews to one place.

No more scouring 20+ sites to ensure you’re aware of every online review that your business receives. Rather, Podium brings all of your reviews, from multiple review sites, into one easy-to-understand dashboard. Sort, filter, see, and respond to, any and every review that your business collects – right from Podium.

Build and manage your online reputation on-the-go.

Are you or your employees constantly on-the-go? No problem. Podium’s iOS and Android app is free for Podium users and allows you to send review invites, manage existing reviews, and see analytics, right from your smartphone. Whether desktop or mobile, Podium allows you to easily build and manage your online reputation from anywhere in the world.

Engage customers via familiar channels.

Podium Messenger enables businesses to interact with customers via familiar channels. Whether you’re responding to feedback, following up on a review invite, or answering general questions, Messenger is a personal and convenient way to engage customers for more face-to-face interactions.

Manage all of your location’s reviews and performance from one place.

Does your business have multiple locations? Podium makes it easy for you to see and compare locations from one screen. Whether you want to see overall star rating, total number of reviews, or the sites that perform best by location, Podium’s reporting tools make it easy.
