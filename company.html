A little bit about PODIUM

Podium is a Utah-based software focused on powering the modern relationship of trust between businesses and customers.
Today, businesses broadcast instead of engage. Podium changes that with the belief that customers are your best form of marketing.
Podium brings marketing, operations, and customer experience into one platform, helping businesses drive user-generated content to better understand customers, increase visibility, and influence purchase decisions. Built to utilize everyday consumer technology, Podium enables you to influence what is said and where it’s said by focusing on the sites and thematics that matter most to your business.

PODIUM’s Partner Program

Podium works with marketing and operations consultants across the globe. With a strategic approach, we help you as a partner create a solution that works best for your individual clients. We’re currently partnering with select consultants and want to talk with you.
